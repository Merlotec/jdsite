Think of something you would really like to buy (you don't actually have to buy it) and research different places where you can buy the item. Compare the prices. What are the highest and lowest prices you could pay for the item? Is the cheapest version too good to be true? Read reviews. Can you find the same thing second hand on a site like ebay or Gumtree etc.? If so, can you message the seller politely to negotiate an even cheaper deal? Don't shy away from asking, they can only say no!
