You can learn to save a lot of money by using coupons and codes. They can be found in newspapers, magazines and online. See if you can find coupons for things you (and/or your family) would normally buy. On what types of things can you save? Find out where you can find coupons and where to use them. Over the course of a month, see how many things on which you can save. For assessment, show photographic evidence of what you have bought and how much of a saving you have made. This should be in the form of a table stating: what your chosen items were, normal price, price with coupon, where you found each coupon.
