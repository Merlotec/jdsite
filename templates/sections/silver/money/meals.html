Dinner can be much cheaper than a take-away meal! Dinner could be things like Macaroni cheese, pasta with a simple sauce, hearty soup, chilli, or a stir fry etc.
Over the course of five weeks, learn how to make five different budget meals. Create a recipe book with the five simple, cheap recipes with photographic evidence of you learning how to cook each one. This could be very useful to you when you leave home!
For assessment, you must bring in the recipe book you have created along with photos.
