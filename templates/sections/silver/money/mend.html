Instead of throwing away broken items or torn clothes and having to spend money on new ones, learn to fix them by hand / machine sewing, darning or, if it is something other than fabric, perhaps by gluing or screwing etc. If a hem has come lose, a button is missing, a seam is ripped, the cuff of a jumper frayed, a leg has fallen off a stool etc., learn to fix it.
For assessment, be able to show your teacher 'before and after' evidence of five things you have fixed.
