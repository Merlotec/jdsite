Using iMovies, Windows Movie Maker or other software, make a video of a past-time that causes no damage or harm and makes you very happy. The video should be around three minutes, perhaps using a suitable song from start to finish. Your film MUST include:
  • a title
  • credits at the end
  • a sound track as well as your voice over (in parts)
  • text appearing on the screen
  • transitions
  • photos
  • a clear idea of what you enjoy doing, why you enjoy it and how it makes you feel.

For assessment you must show your self assessment and your completed video to your Duke Leader.
